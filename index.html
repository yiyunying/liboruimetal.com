Global steel supplier

Shandong Laboratory Metal Co, Ltd. has a proud history of over a decade in the international trade sector, specializing in the production of forged steel products. Over the years, we have established a comprehensive service chain that encompasses melting, forging, heat treatment, surface treatment, and logistics. This integrated approach has allowed us to cater to a diverse range of clients both domestically and internationally.

International Procurement Service

At International Procurement Service, we offer comprehensive procurement services tailored to meet the demands of todays global marketplace. With a robust network of customers around the world, from America to Europe and Asia, we pride ourselves on offering unparalleled worldwide service and delivering excellence wherever you might be located.
In the realm of global procurement, navigating the complexities of sourcing from international markets can be a daunting task. At Laboratory, we serve as your trusted eyes in China, offering comprehensive procurement services tailored to your specific needs.
When it comes to selecting new suppliers, the stakes are high. Finding the right partner can make all the difference in terms of product quality, cost-effectiveness, and overall supply chain efficiency. However, conducting due diligence on potential suppliers in a foreign market presents its own set of challenges.
This is where we come in. With our extensive network and deep-rooted expertise in the Chinese market, we specialize in researching and vetting manufacturers on your behalf. Whether you’re seeking to expand your supplier base or exploring new product lines, we’ll leverage our local knowledge and industry insights to identify reputable and reliable partners that align with your requirements.
